Steak

Description

Vegans BEGONE!

Ingredients

  • NY Strip Steak
  • Kosher Salt
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Salt steaks and bring to room temp
  2. Bring pan to medium-high heat
  3. Add oil and then steak to pan
  4. Sear for three minutes on each side
  5. Let rest 5-10 minutes and enjoy!
